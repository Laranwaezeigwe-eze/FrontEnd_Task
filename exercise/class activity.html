Castle

Magic Kingdom, Walt Disney World

Photo collation by: Lara Cruft

Built in 1971, the castle is designed to reflect the late-Gothic, flamboyant style of the 1400s.
There are a total of 27 towers on the castle.The tower with the clock in front is number 10,
the tallest is number 20. Number 23 is the other golden-roofed tower. Cinderella Castle is also
surrounded by a moat, which contains approximately 3.37 million US gallons (12,800 m3) of water
